6. Market Efficiency (when markets work well)

8. Price Floor (government intervention - above equilibrium)
9. Tax Incidence (who really pays taxes)

## Social Media Misinformation Targets

- "Company X is greedy - that's why prices are high" (ignoring supply/demand)
- "Rent control keeps housing affordable" (ignoring shortage effects)
- "Price gouging laws protect consumers" (ignoring allocation problems)
- "Minimum wage increases don't cause unemployment" (depends on magnitude and market)
- "The government should just control prices" (ignoring efficiency losses)
