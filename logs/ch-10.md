- Rate on durability, portability, divisibility, uniformity, scarcity, acceptability
- Reveal why some things make better money than others

**2. Fractional Reserve Money Multiplier**
- Bloom Level: Apply (L3)
- Start with initial deposit
- Watch it multiply through banking system
- Adjust reserve ratio to see effect on money creation
- Key insight: Banks create money through lending!

**3. Fed Interest Rate Simulator**
- Bloom Level: Apply (L3)
- Adjust federal funds rate up/down
- See ripple effects: mortgage rates, car loans, savings rates
- Show impact on borrowing, spending, inflation, unemployment
- Lag effects visualization
