# Chapter 2: Getting Started with Economic Thinking

## The Economic Way of Thinking

Economists approach problems systematically using models and data.

## Key Principles

1. **People respond to incentives**
2. **Trade creates value**
3. **Markets coordinate activity**
4. **Costs and benefits matter at the margin**

## Economic Models

Economic models simplify reality to help us understand complex relationships.

### The Production Possibilities Frontier

The PPF shows the trade-offs a society faces when producing two goods.
