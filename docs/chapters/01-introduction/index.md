# Chapter 1: Introduction to Economics

## What is Economics?

Economics is the study of how individuals, businesses, and governments make choices about allocating scarce resources to satisfy unlimited wants.

## Key Concepts

- **Scarcity** - Limited resources vs. unlimited wants
- **Opportunity Cost** - The value of the next best alternative
- **Trade-offs** - Giving up one thing to get another

## Why Study Economics?

Understanding economics helps us:

1. Make better personal financial decisions
2. Understand government policies
3. Analyze business strategies
4. Interpret news and current events
